RADS TEXAS LLC

RADS TEXAS is a real estate investment company based in the Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW) metroplex. The company specializes in acquiring, managing, and leasing residential and multi-family properties across high-demand neighborhoods within the region. Our mission is to build long-term value through strategic property acquisitions and quality rental management, providing comfortable, well-maintained housing options for tenants while delivering steady returns for stakeholders. With a focus on market trends, community development, and responsible ownership, RADS TEXAS aims to grow a diversified real estate portfolio that supports both short-term cash flow and long-term asset appreciation in the dynamic DFW market.
